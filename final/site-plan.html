Individual Project Site Plan

Proposed website: Sydney Coastal Trails — a compact guide to Sydney’s best seaside walks, lookouts, and practical tips. Author: Ivan Diaz de Sandi Alvarez (from Mexico, living in Sydney, Australia).

Planning Document

Site Name

Sydney Coastal Trails

This name is short, descriptive, and clear about the focus: discovering and planning coastal walks in Sydney. It aligns well with search intent (e.g., “Sydney coastal walks”, “best coastal trails in Sydney”).

Optional domain idea: sydneycoastaltrails.com (or .au if available)

Site Purpose

Provide a friendly hub for locals and visitors to explore Sydney’s coastal walks: trail highlights, difficulty, distance, facilities, transport tips, and safety/weather notes. Users can browse featured trails, save favorites (local storage), and view trail details in a modal.

Scenarios (User Questions)

  • “Which beginner-friendly coastal trails can I do in under 2 hours near Bondi?”
  • “What’s the current weather and 3-day outlook for a Saturday morning walk?”
  • “Do any trails have public transport access and coffee shops nearby?”

Body — Inter (400/500)

Clear, modern text for paragraphs, captions, and lists.

Used for: body copy, lists, buttons, and small UI text.

Why these fonts?

Poppins provides approachable, rounded headings that pair well with map/travel content. Inter is highly readable on mobile and desktop and remains visually neutral.
